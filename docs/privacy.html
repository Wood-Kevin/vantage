Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Developer: Kevin Wood

Your privacy is important. This policy explains what information Vantage does and does not collect.

No Personal Data Collected

Vantage does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No account is required to use the app.

Everything Stays on Your Device

All calculations are performed locally on your device. Any data you enter into the app — numbers, measurements, dates — never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

  • No accounts or sign-in required
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising SDKs

In-App Purchases

Premium access is available as an optional purchase. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store. Vantage does not receive, store, or process your payment information.

No Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. There are no integrations with social networks, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

Vantage does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Because no data is collected from any user, this applies equally to all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
