Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026  ·  App: Vantage

🔒

The short version

Vantage does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no servers. Everything stays on your device.

1. No Personal Data Collected

Vantage does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal details. There is no registration or account system of any kind.

2. On-Device Storage Only

Any data you enter into the app — such as water intake counts or subscription names — is stored exclusively on your device using SQLite, a local database that never leaves your phone. This data is never uploaded, synced, or shared with any external server or service.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

3. No Third-Party SDKs or Analytics

Vantage does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. The app contains only the code necessary to provide its calculator and utility features.

  • No analytics SDK
  • No advertising SDK
  • No crash reporting service
  • No payment processor
  • No social media integration

4. No Network Requests

Vantage makes no network requests during normal operation. All calculations are performed locally on your device. The app does not connect to the internet, fetch remote data, or communicate with any server. It works fully offline from the moment it is installed.

5. Children's Privacy

Vantage does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Because the app collects no data from any user at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

6. App Store Platforms

Vantage is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Those platforms may collect certain usage data (such as download counts or crash reports) according to their own privacy policies. This data is collected by Apple or Google directly and is governed by their respective policies, not this one.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above and, where required by law, notify users through the App Store update notes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
